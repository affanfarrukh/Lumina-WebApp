Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reschedule a booking?

Go to My Bookings, find your upcoming appointment, and tap 'Reschedule' to pick a new date and time.

Can I cancel for free?

Cancellations made 24 hours before the appointment are free. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

How do I change my stylist?

During the booking flow, you can browse and select a preferred stylist before confirming.
